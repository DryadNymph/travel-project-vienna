Why Vienna?

Vienna, the city of classical elegance and grandeur

Vienna captivates visitors with its timeless charm and majestic atmosphere. Steeped in history, its streets echo with the footsteps of emperors and composers, while its palaces stand as testaments to a bygone era of opulence. From the enchanting melodies of Mozart and Strauss to the magnificent architecture of the Hofburg Palace and the Schönbrunn Palace, Vienna exudes a sense of refined sophistication. Strolling along the cobblestone streets, one is enveloped in the rich tapestry of cultural heritage, where each building tells a story of artistic brilliance and imperial grandeur. With its graceful parks, regal avenues, and world-renowned coffee houses, Vienna remains a beacon of classical elegance, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its timeless allure.

My favorite cafés in Vienna

Café Sacher

Indulge in the Viennese café culture with a slice of the Original Sacher-Torte at Café Sacher Wien. Book a table at the iconic coffee house in Vienna.

Address:

Philharmoniker Str. 4, 1010 Wien, Austria

What I like about it

Its delicious and infamous Sacher Torte, a rich chocolate cake with a layer of apricot jam, its cozy and elegant design and the tradition it represens in Vienna.

Demel Wien

Demel isn’t just a coffee house, it’s a cultural institution with centuries of artisanal baking knowledge.

Address:

Kohlmarkt 14, 1010 Wien, Austria

What I like about it

Its exquisite pastries, elegant ambiance, and rich history dating back to the 18th century, its traditional Viennese coffeehouse experience, as well as the high-quality dessert and chocolates.

Aida Café Konditorei

Enjoy the best coffee in Vienna. Visit any Aida café in the historic city and sample the very best Viennese coffee from the world-famous coffee company.

Address:

Wollzeile 28, 1010 Wien, Austria

What I like about it

Its affordable yet very tasty pastries, cakes, and coffee, its casual atmosphere and the opportunity to indulge in sweet treats without breaking the bank.
